Zhibo He

MSc Student, Security & Resilience·Imperial College London

I am a master’s student in Security and Resilience: Science and Technology at Imperial College London. I work on program analysis and software testing — currently my MSc thesis on differential testing, supervised by Prof. Cristian Cadar.

My interests span program analysis, software testing, differential fuzzing, symbolic execution, software reliability, AI-assisted program reasoning, and LLMs for code and security. Previously I completed a BSc in Information Management and Information Systems at Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (First Class Honours, departmental top 5%). The best way to reach me is by email.
